Instructions

1. Search for the Group you would like to analyse into more dept bellow
2. Click on the corresponding image of the group
3. Wait until all the observations with the corresponding centroinds are loaded
4. Once the observations are loaded, click on the observation you want to analyse
5. Wait until the plot is loaded on the right hand side
6. If you want to analyse a different observation, just click on another one at the same page
7. If you want to start again, click on "Home" in the menue
