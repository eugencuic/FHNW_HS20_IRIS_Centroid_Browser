Instructions

1. Search for the Group you would like to analyse into more dept bellow
2. Click on the corresponding image of the group
3. Wait until all the observations with the corresponding centroinds are loaded
4. Once the observations are loaded, click on the observation you want to analyse
5. Wait until the plots areloaded
6. Check the high level summary on the plot on righ hand side|
7. Check the more detailed plots with slits bellow the highlevel plot
8. If you want to analyse a different observation from the same centroid, just click on another one at the same page
9. If you want to start again, click on "Home" in the menue
